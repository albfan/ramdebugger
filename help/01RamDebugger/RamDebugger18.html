Additional packages

RamDebugger needs several packages to work properly. Other packages are just included to give additional functionality to the user. All the needed packages are included in the distribution as a convenience to the final user.

Some of the packages have been modified, either to fix some errors or to improve some capabilities. The packages that have been modified are marked below.
