FAQ

Q: On Windows, RamDebugger will not start, with an error related to loading/executing a library from a temporal directory.

A: Try to disconnect your antivirus software

Q: TCL does not find some TCL packages that are available in my normal TCL distribution

A: You need to add the directories where the packages are located to the ::auto_path variable. Either:

  1. Add the directory to: Edit->Preferences->Directories
  1. Add a line to your TCL script: lappend ::auto_path $my_directory
